Our Mission

Our organization pursues several goals that can be identified as our mission. Learn more about them below.

  • Saving Children: Our main mission is to save and rescue permanently displaced children.
  • Peace On The Planet: By working with our partners, we aim to establish peaceful relationships.
  • Care & Protection: We provide global care and protection to support children all over the world.
